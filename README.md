This repository is a central hub for my work in risk analytics, bringing together projects that explore data-driven approaches to risk assessment, monitoring, and decision support.
Projects Included
1. Strategic Recon: 
- Focuses on identifying, analyzing, and mapping potential risk factors.
- Provides insights that support proactive risk management and long-term planning.
2. Data Quality Dashboard
- Designed to monitor and visualize the integrity of datasets used in risk analysis.
- Tracks key metrics such as completeness, accuracy, consistency, and timeliness.
- Helps ensure that risk models and analytics are built on reliable, high-quality data.
Purpose
The goal of this repository is to demonstrate practical applications of risk analytics, combining strategic foresight with data governance.
Each project highlights methods to improve decision-making by reducing uncertainty and strengthening confidence in the underlying data.
